# Career OS
## Product Requirements Document (PRD)
### Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the purpose, vision, scope, and success criteria for Career OS.

It serves as the authoritative product definition for the project and should guide every future design, architectural, and development decision.

This document intentionally avoids implementation details. Technical architecture, data models, repository structure, and user interface specifications are documented separately.

---

# Product Overview

Career OS is a personal professional intelligence platform that centralizes, organizes, and manages professional knowledge throughout an individual's career.

Rather than maintaining disconnected resumes, portfolio documents, interview notes, certifications, case studies, and professional profiles, Career OS provides a single source of truth that powers every career-related workflow.

Career OS transforms fragmented professional information into structured, reusable knowledge that can be applied consistently across multiple career outputs.

---

# Vision

To build a permanent professional intelligence platform that enables professionals to manage their careers from a single, structured knowledge base.

Career OS should become the foundation for every professional asset, allowing career knowledge to be reused, refined, and expanded over time instead of repeatedly recreated.

The platform is designed to remain valuable regardless of changing technologies, hiring platforms, or AI tools.

---

# Mission

Enable professionals to organize, maintain, and leverage their experience through structured knowledge that supports career growth, job acquisition, freelancing, professional branding, and continuous development.

---

# Problem Statement

Professional information is typically scattered across numerous disconnected documents and platforms, including resumes, portfolios, LinkedIn profiles, freelance marketplaces, certifications, interview notes, and project documentation.

This fragmented approach creates several challenges:

- Duplicate information across multiple documents.
- Inconsistent professional branding.
- Time-consuming maintenance.
- Repeated rewriting of the same accomplishments.
- Difficulty locating relevant experience.
- Loss of institutional career knowledge over time.
- Inefficient preparation for new opportunities.

As professional experience grows, maintaining consistency becomes increasingly difficult.

---

# Proposed Solution

Career OS provides a centralized professional intelligence platform where career knowledge is maintained once and reused across multiple applications.

Instead of treating resumes, portfolios, and professional profiles as independent documents, Career OS treats them as outputs generated from a common knowledge foundation.

Every professional asset references the same underlying information, ensuring consistency, accuracy, and maintainability.

---

# Target User

## Primary User

Version 1 is designed for an individual professional who actively manages their career.

Typical use cases include:

- Job applications
- Freelancing
- Professional networking
- Interview preparation
- Portfolio management
- Career planning
- Continuous learning

Although initially designed for a single user, the platform should remain architecturally extensible for future expansion.

---

# Product Goals

Career OS should enable users to:

- Maintain a single source of professional truth.
- Organize career knowledge in a structured manner.
- Reduce repetitive work.
- Improve consistency across professional platforms.
- Retrieve relevant professional evidence quickly.
- Support multiple career workflows from one knowledge base.
- Preserve professional knowledge as experience grows.

---

# Core Principles

## Single Source of Truth

Every professional fact should exist only once.

Updates should automatically benefit every career workflow that references the information.

---

## Structured Knowledge

Professional information should be organized into reusable knowledge rather than isolated documents whenever practical.

---

## Reusability

Career knowledge should support multiple outputs without duplication.

---

## Consistency

Every professional platform should communicate the same personal brand, experience, and value proposition.

---

## Maintainability

Career knowledge should become easier to maintain as it grows rather than more difficult.

---

## Portability

Professional knowledge should remain portable and independent of specific platforms or services.

---

## Longevity

Career OS should remain useful throughout an entire professional career rather than supporting only immediate job applications.

---

# Functional Scope

Career OS supports professional workflows including:

- Career knowledge management
- Resume preparation
- Cover letter preparation
- Interview preparation
- Portfolio management
- Professional profile management
- Proposal preparation
- Career planning
- Professional documentation

These workflows are powered by the same underlying professional knowledge.

---

# Out of Scope (Version 1)

The following capabilities are intentionally excluded from Version 1:

- Multi-user collaboration
- User authentication
- Payment systems
- Recruitment CRM
- Employer-facing portals
- Social networking functionality
- Automated job applications
- Mobile-native applications

Future versions may introduce additional capabilities without changing the core product philosophy.

---

# Success Criteria

Career OS will be considered successful when it enables users to:

- Maintain one authoritative professional knowledge base.
- Produce consistent career assets across multiple platforms.
- Significantly reduce the effort required to prepare professional materials.
- Quickly retrieve relevant professional evidence.
- Preserve professional knowledge over the long term.
- Expand career information without increasing maintenance complexity.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Career OS is intended to evolve into a comprehensive professional intelligence platform that supports every stage of a modern career.

Future versions may extend the platform with additional services, workflows, and integrations while continuing to follow its foundational principle:

> Build professional knowledge once. Reuse it everywhere.
